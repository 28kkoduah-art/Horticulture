Info

    

    The Taconic High School Horticulture Program proudly offers a variety of high-quality, student-grown plants for sale throughout the year. All plants are grown and maintained by students as part of their hands-on Career & Technical Education (CTE) experience.
Plant sales are an important part of the horticulture curriculum, giving students real-world experience in greenhouse production, plant care, and customer service. Proceeds from sales help support the program, purchase supplies, and enhance student learning opportunities.
