    Activites - Horticulture Webpage
    
    
























  

    
Cultivation is an activity that involves preparing and managing soil to grow crops or plants. It includes tasks such as tilling the soil, planting seeds, watering, removing weeds, and caring for plants as they grow. The goal of cultivation is to create the best possible conditions for healthy plant growth and successful harvests.
